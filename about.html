My Story

I am a website developer who graduated from SMKN 1 Cirebon and I am interested in the programming world. I started learning this field in my second year of high school and deepened my knowledge during my internship at PT. Nusabot Inovasi Teknologi.

I once participated in a Student Competency Competition in the field of Web Technologies and achieved first place at the city level. On several occasions, I also served as a mentor assistant during Coding Day events, where I was responsible for troubleshooting errors encountered by Coding Day participants.

After graduating, I obtained LSP and BNSP Certificates. Following that, I attended a one-month Online Frontend Developer Bootcamp and successfully completed the training.
